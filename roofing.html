Overview

Protecting Your Home from the Top Down

Your roof is your property's first line of defence against the Lancashire weather. Craig Kershaw Ltd provides durable, professional roofing solutions for homeowners, landlords and social housing providers across the region.

From full re-roofs and new-build roofing through to repairs, flat roofing and gutter replacements, we deliver work that protects your home for decades. All our roofing is installed to current building regulations with quality materials and skilled craftsmanship.

Roofing Capabilities

Tile Roofing

Flat Roofing

Common Questions

Roofing FAQ

What colour and style options are available? +
We offer a wide range of tile profiles, colours and materials to suit any property style. From traditional slate-effect tiles to modern interlocking concrete, we can match your existing roof or help you choose something new. For flat roofing, we offer several membrane colours and finishes. We will bring samples so you can see and feel the materials before committing.
Are there any hidden costs in your roofing quotes? +
No. Our quotes cover everything: scaffolding, materials, labour, waste removal and any necessary building control fees. If we discover unexpected issues once the old roof is stripped (such as rotten timbers), we discuss the additional work and cost with you before proceeding. There are never any surprise charges.
How do you minimise disruption during a re-roof? +
We plan roofing works carefully to minimise disruption. Scaffolding is erected and removed promptly, and we ensure your property is weatherproofed at the end of each working day. We keep your garden and driveway protected with sheeting and clear all debris daily. Most standard re-roofs are completed within one to two weeks depending on size and weather.
What safety measures do you have in place? +
Safety is paramount on every roofing job. We use full scaffolding with guard rails, carry out risk assessments before work begins, and our team is trained in working at height procedures. We are fully insured and comply with all current health and safety regulations. We also take care to protect members of the public and neighbouring properties.
