Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of social housing work do you carry out?
+
We carry out a wide range of social housing construction work including DDA-compliant adaptations (wet rooms, ramps, widened doorways), extensions, window and door replacement programmes, roofing, kitchen and bathroom refurbishments, plastering, and general maintenance and repair works.
Which local authorities do you work with?
+
We have worked with Burnley Borough Council for over 20 years and have experience working with other local authorities and housing associations across Lancashire and West Yorkshire. We are happy to discuss new partnerships and can provide references from existing public sector clients.
How do you minimise disruption to tenants?
+
We communicate with tenants before, during, and after each project. Our team works efficiently to minimise the length of disruption, keeps all work areas clean and tidy, and takes care to protect tenants' belongings. We schedule noisy or disruptive work at considerate times and always leave the property clean on completion.
Are your team DBS checked and fully insured?
+
Yes. All team members working on social housing projects are DBS checked. We carry comprehensive public liability insurance and employer's liability insurance. We are fully compliant with all health and safety regulations and can provide documentation upon request.
