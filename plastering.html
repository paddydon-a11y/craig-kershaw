FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you protect my home during plastering work? +
We use protective sheets to cover floors, furniture, and fixtures before any work begins. Our team works cleanly and efficiently, ensuring your home is fully protected throughout the project and left tidy at the end of each day.
How long does plaster take to dry? +
Fresh plaster can take up to 4 weeks to fully dry, depending on the thickness of the application and ventilation in the room. We recommend waiting until the plaster has turned a uniform light pink colour before painting or wallpapering.
Are there any hidden costs? +
No. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. The quote we provide covers everything discussed and agreed upon. There are no hidden charges or surprise fees — the price we quote is the price you pay.
How much disruption will there be? +
We keep disruption to an absolute minimum. Our team is respectful of your home and daily routine, working efficiently and maintaining a clean, safe site throughout. We treat every property as if it were our own.
