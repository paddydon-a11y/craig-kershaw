Common Questions

Windows & Doors FAQ

What type of window is best for my property? +
It depends on your property style, budget and priorities. uPVC is the most popular choice for its low cost and excellent insulation. Aluminium suits modern builds and extensions with its slim, sleek profiles. We will survey your property and recommend the best option for your needs — free of charge.
Will new windows really reduce my energy bills? +
Yes. Modern double and triple glazed windows significantly reduce heat loss compared to older single-glazed or failing sealed units. Most homeowners notice a meaningful reduction in their energy bills, especially in older properties. All our windows are rated for energy efficiency to current building standards.
Are there any hidden costs in your quotes? +
No. Our quotes include supply, delivery, installation, removal of old frames, and all finishing work. We provide a fully itemised breakdown so you can see exactly where your money is going. There are no call-out fees and no surprises.
How do you protect my home during installation? +
We use dust sheets throughout, carefully remove old frames to minimise mess, and ensure your property is fully weatherproofed at the end of each day. Most standard window replacements are completed within a single day, keeping disruption to an absolute minimum.
