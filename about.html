A Trusted Partner Since 1991

For over 33 years, Craig Kershaw has been a trusted construction partner throughout the North West. Two generations of Kershaws, one unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Story

Built on Family Values

Craig Kershaw Ltd was founded in 1991 by Craig Kershaw. Having worked in the construction industry for years, he identified the challenges homeowners face when undertaking building work: coordinating multiple tradespeople, navigating planning permissions, and ensuring safety standards are met.

He established the company to provide comprehensive construction services under one contractor — one team, one point of contact, from design to completion. This simple idea became the foundation of a business that has thrived for over three decades.

Today, the company is led by Brad Kershaw, Craig's son, who grew up on building sites and learned the trade from the ground up. Brad continues the family legacy with the same commitment to honest pricing, quality workmanship, and treating every project — large or small — with the same care and professionalism.
